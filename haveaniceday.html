Have a Nice Day

Live-Data Sculpture

Installation | Coding, Sculpture

Initially programmed to pull live data off of the internet to display physically, this cloud sculpture started as a experiment to learn arduino and processing technology as a weather forecast infographic. But at the start of the day, instead of finding out if it is or is not optimal conditions before anything else, you could see a different kind of information through color and light â€” the potential of the day to be something more.

Have a Nice Day is a sculpture that uses behavioral psychology in order to change one's perspective on and at the start of each day, and eventually fortify the neural connections that initiate and perpetuate positive thinking.
