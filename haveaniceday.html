Have a Nice Day

Live-Data Scultpure

3-D | Coding, Building, Digital

Initially programmed to pull live data off of the internet to display physically, this cloud was a weather forecast infographic.

But at the start of the day, instead of finding out if it's not an optimal day before it has begun, you could see a different kind of information through color and light. You could see the potential of the day to be something more.

"You have to regard yourself as a cloud in the flesh, because you see clouds never make mistakes. Did you ever see a cloud that was misshapen? Did you ever see a badly designed wave? No, they always do the right thing." Alan Watts
