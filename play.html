Play

An Ungergrad Thesis

Design Research and Experimentation

Beginning with questions about how to live a creatively fulfilling life, or rather, have fun with creating and maintaining a creative and joyful lifestyle, I began research on the topic of play and creativity. Through research on artists and experiments, through making art and conducting experiments, I am forever seeking playful experiences for myself and others to find the beauty in mistakes, the magic of Wabi-Sabi, and the joy and stimulation of being playful.

Can spontaneos play encourage craetivity and stop us from inhibiting our natural creative responses to life? Hoping to spread playfulness to others, my message to all that we are all creative creatures exists in the form of an encouraging service that helps us all transform into our creative creatures selves.
