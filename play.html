Play

An Ungergrad Thesis

Design Research and Experimentation

Beginning with questions about how to live a creatively fulfilling life, or rather, have fun with creating and maintaining a creative and joyful lifestyle, I began research on the topic of play and creativity.

Through research on artists and experiments, through making art and conducting experiments, I am forever seeking playful experiences for myself and others to find the beauty in mistakes, the magic of Wabi-Sabi, and the joy and stimulation of being playful.

Hoping to spread playfulness to others, my message to all that we are all creative creatures exists in the form of a transition into actual creative creatures.
