Here & Now

Getting Nowhere

Artist Book | Print, Production

An experience similar to Cage's music based on our shared beliefs on non-dualism and achieving "at-oneness" or experiencing the present moment by removing human constructs out of normal situations.

Consisting of John Cage's Lecture on Nothing & Lecture on Something, annotated with gold foiled Henry David Thoreau's thoughts on philosophy, time, and experience.
